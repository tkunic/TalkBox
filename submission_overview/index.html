Submission Overview

TalkBox I is an open-source, open-hardware Speech Generating Device (SGD). It provides the community with a way to easily access a low-cost, easily configurable SGD. It also provides employment opportunities for high school students who have developmental disabilities.

A submission to the 2014 IDeA Competition.
